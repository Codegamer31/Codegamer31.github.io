WHO WE ARE?

We are a Brazilian-origin company, located in Boston, that strives to offer our customers an exceptional experience with authentic Brazilian açaí. We are a passionate team committed to providing our customers with the best product possible, using only the freshest and highest quality ingredients. From the beginning, our goal has been to bring the flavor and energy of Brazil to the Boston City, and we are proud to have earned a great reputation in the local community. We believe that our commitment to quality, safety, and taste sets us apart, and we are excited to continue to grow and serve our audience. Come experience the best of Brazilian acai with Açaí Pocket!

OUR PRODUCTS

Aiming to prioritize our products for the health of our customers, we produce our products without the use of transgenic ingredients bringing more safety to health, besides using an ideal soil for the production of açaí, we also use a sustainable process to leave our açaís with more nutrients for health!
